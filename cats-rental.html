About Us

Welcome to the world's largest network of cat lovers!

We believe everyone deserves the unconditional love of a pet - and at KITTIES TO GO!, our mission is to make it easier to experience that love.

Founded in 2011, KITTIES TO GO! app and website connect cat parents with loving pet sitters in the neighborhood across the State of Washington.

We empower our community of trusted pet sitters to run their own pet care business on KITTIES TO GO! with the tools and security of a global company to back them.
